Privacy Policy

At leunre.in, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and enroll in our courses.

Information We Collect: When you register or enroll in a course, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, payment details, and other relevant information such as educational background, preferences, and course interests. We may collect information about how you use our website, such as pages visited and time spent on the site.

How We Use Your Information: We use your information to process your course enrollment and payments, communicate with you about courses, updates, and support and Improve our website and learning materials.

Sharing Your Information: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in delivering our courses (e.g., payment processors), but only to the extent necessary.

Data Security: We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. While we strive to secure your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Cookies & Tracking: Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how visitors use the site and improve the learning experience.

Third-Party Links: Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites.

Your Rights: You can update or correct your personal information at any time by contacting us via email at in.leunre@gmail.com. You can also request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

Updates to Privacy Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review our Privacy Policy page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

By using our website and enrolling in courses, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
